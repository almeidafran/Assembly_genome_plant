# Not implemented here #

##### Haplotig purging #####

This is necessary or recommended when you want a haploid (collapsed) assembly—representing a single copy per genomic region—and the organism exhibits sufficient 
heterozygosity to generate artificial duplications.

However, it may not be necessary if you are creating a haplotype-resolved assembly (i.e., separating paternal and maternal haplotypes).

##### Polishing #####

With PacBio HiFi data, polishing is generally less critical than with assemblies generated using PacBio CLR or Oxford Nanopore, as HiFi reads already possess 
high accuracy. Nevertheless, it can still be performed to correct residual errors, particularly indels or minor inconsistencies.
